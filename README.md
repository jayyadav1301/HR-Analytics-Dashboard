HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce trends, employee demographics, and attrition patterns to support data-driven HR decision making. The dashboard analyzes 1,470 employees and tracks critical workforce metrics such as attrition rate, job satisfaction, and demographic distribution.

Key Workforce Metrics
- The dashboard highlights major HR KPIs including:
- Total Employees: 1,470
- Active Employees: 1,233
- Total Attrition: 237 employees
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Average Employee Age: 36.92 years
These metrics allow HR teams to quickly evaluate workforce stability and retention levels.

Attrition Insights
- Department-level analysis shows that R&D experiences the highest attrition, followed by Sales, while HR has the lowest attrition levels. This  helps HR leaders identify departments where employee retention strategies may be required.
- Attrition trends are also analyzed across education fields, where employees from Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds show higher attrition counts, suggesting possible skill demand shifts or career mobility patterns.

Demographic Analysis
- The dashboard provides a detailed breakdown of employees across age groups and gender, revealing that the 25–34 age group represents the largest portion of the workforce. Attrition patterns across age groups indicate that younger employees tend to leave more frequently, which may highlight early-career mobility.

Job Satisfaction Analysis
- A job satisfaction matrix analyzes employee sentiment across different roles such as Sales Executive, Research Scientist, Laboratory Technician, and Managers. This helps HR identify which job roles experience lower satisfaction levels and may require engagement or policy improvements.

Workforce Planning Metrics
- Additional indicators such as new applications (700) and layoffs (130) provide insights into hiring pipeline and workforce restructuring activities, supporting HR planning and talent management.
Business Impact

This dashboard enables HR teams to:
- Identify high attrition departments and demographics
- Monitor employee satisfaction across job roles
- Support strategic hiring and retention planning
- Improve HR reporting efficiency through interactive visual analytics
- The dashboard is built using Power BI with DAX measures and interactive slicers, allowing dynamic filtering by age, gender, marital status, and -job role for deeper workforce insights.
